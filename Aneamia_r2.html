Lentil Soup with Carrots and Kale

Serves: 4 Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes

High in plant-based protein, fiber, and iron, perfect for boosting energy levels.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 bunch kale, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 250 kcal
Iron: 6.5 mg
Protein: 14 g
Vitamin C: 40 mg
Fiber: 12 g

Tools:

  • Large pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and cumin, saut√© for 5 minutes.
  2. Add carrots, lentils, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Add kale and cook for another 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve hot.
