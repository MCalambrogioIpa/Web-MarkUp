Grilled Salmon with Lemon and Herbs

Serves: 2 Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids from salmon to support heart health, with herbs and lemon adding flavor without extra fat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs (dill, thyme, or rosemary)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 350 kcal
Protein: 35 g
Fiber: 0 g
Saturated Fat: 1.5 g
Cholesterol: 75 mg

Tools:

  • Grill
  • Brush
  • Tongs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush salmon with olive oil and season with herbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill salmon for 6-7 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
  4. Serve with lemon slices.
