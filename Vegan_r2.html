Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Serves: 4 Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes

Portobello mushrooms are low in calories and high in antioxidants, while quinoa provides protein and fiber.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 220 kcal
Protein: 8 g
Fiber: 5 g
Iron: 2 mg
Vitamin C: 30 mg

Tools:

  • Oven
  • Baking sheet
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Clean mushrooms and remove stems.
  2. In a bowl, mix quinoa, sun-dried tomatoes, feta cheese, garlic, and balsamic vinegar.
  3. Stuff mushrooms with the mixture and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes. Serve warm.
