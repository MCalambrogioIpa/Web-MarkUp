Grilled Chicken with Quinoa and Broccoli

Serves: 2 Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes

Packed with protein, iron, and vitamin C, supporting muscle and immune function.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 450 kcal
Iron: 4 mg
Protein: 40 g
Vitamin C: 70 mg
Fiber: 6 g

Tools:

  • Grill or pan
  • Pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper and grill for about 6-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked.
  2. Cook quinoa according to package instructions (about 15 minutes).
  3. Steam the broccoli for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the broccoli. Serve with the chicken and quinoa.
