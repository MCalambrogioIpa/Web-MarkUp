Lentil and Vegetable Shepherd’s Pie

Serves: 4 Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 45 minutes

Lentils and vegetables provide fiber and protein, while mashed potatoes add comfort and additional nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green lentils
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups mashed potatoes (prepared with skim milk)
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 320 kcal
Protein: 12 g
Fiber: 10 g
Iron: 4 mg
Vitamin C: 25 mg

Tools:

  • Pot
  • Pan
  • Baking dish
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. Cook lentils in vegetable broth until tender (about 30 minutes). Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onions, garlic, carrots, and peas until softened.
  3. Mix lentils into the vegetables. Transfer to a baking dish.
  4. Spread mashed potatoes over the lentil mixture.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Serve hot.
