Iron-Rich Lentil Salad

Serves: 2 Time: 30 min

This lentil salad is rich in iron, folate, and vitamin C, perfect for improving red blood cell production and combating anemia.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/2 cup spinach (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup diced red peppers
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup parsley (chopped)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 250 kcal
Carbohydrates: 35g
Protein: 15g
Iron: 6mg
Vitamin C: 25mg
Fiber: 12g

Tools:

  • Large bowl
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Spoon

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine lentils, spinach, red peppers, cherry tomatoes, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the lentil mixture and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
