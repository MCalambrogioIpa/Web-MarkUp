Butternut Squash and Black Bean Tacos

Serves: 4 Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes

Butternut squash provides Vitamin A and fiber, while black beans add protein and additional fiber.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups butternut squash, cubed
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1/2 cup salsa
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 280 kcal
Protein: 9 g
Fiber: 8 g
Iron: 3 mg
Vitamin C: 50 mg

Tools:

  • Oven
  • Baking sheet
  • Pan
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss butternut squash with olive oil, cumin, and paprika. Roast for 20 minutes.
  2. Warm tortillas in a pan or oven.
  3. Fill tortillas with roasted squash, black beans, and salsa. Serve immediately.
