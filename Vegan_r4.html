Kale and Quinoa Salad

Serves: 4 Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes (for quinoa)

Quinoa adds protein and fiber, while kale offers iron and Vitamin C. This salad is refreshing and nutrient-dense.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 3 cups kale, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 310 kcal
Protein: 10 g
Fiber: 7 g
Iron: 4 mg
Vitamin C: 60 mg

Tools:

  • Bowl
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. Toss quinoa, kale, cherry tomatoes, and red onion in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Mix well.
  3. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
