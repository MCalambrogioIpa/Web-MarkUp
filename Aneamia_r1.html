Aneamia

Spinach and Beef Stir-Fry

Serves: 4 Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes

Rich in iron and vitamin C, supporting iron absorption and muscle health.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lean beef, sliced
  • 4 cups spinach
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 320 kcal
Iron: 5 mg
Protein: 26 g
Vitamin C: 35 mg
Fiber: 4 g

Tools:

  • Pan or wok
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic and beef strips, stir-fry until browned (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add bell pepper and cook for another 3 minutes.
  4. Add spinach and soy sauce, cooking for 2-3 minutes until wilted.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
