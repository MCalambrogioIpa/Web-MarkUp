Oatmeal with Berries and Almonds

Serves: 2 Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes

High in soluble fiber from oats to lower LDL cholesterol, with almonds providing heart-healthy fats and berries adding antioxidants.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups water or low-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries)
  • 2 tablespoons sliced almonds
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
Nutritional Info:
Calories: 250 kcal
Protein: 6 g
Fiber: 7 g
Saturated Fat: 0.5 g
Cholesterol: 0 mg

Tools:

  • Pot
  • Spoon
  • Bowl

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, bring water or milk to a boil.
  2. Stir in oats and reduce heat. Simmer for about 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Top with berries, almonds, honey, and cinnamon before serving.
