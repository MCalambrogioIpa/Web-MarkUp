Cauliflower Fried Rice

Serves: 3-4 Time: 20 min

Cauliflower is low in carbs and sugars, and this dish provides a great source of fiber, which helps slow down the absorption of sugars in the bloodstream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of cauliflower (grated into rice-sized pieces)
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/4 cup diced carrots
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 eggs, scrambled (optional)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Nutritional Info:
Calories: ~220 kcal
Carbohydrates: 20g
Sugar: 6g
Protein: 8g
Fat: 10g
Fiber: 5g

Tools:

  • Large pan
  • Grater or food processor
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  2. Add the bell pepper, peas, and carrots to the pan, and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the grated cauliflower and stir to combine. Cook for another 4-5 minutes until the cauliflower is tender.
  4. Stir in the soy sauce and scrambled eggs (if using). Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve as a side or a main dish.
