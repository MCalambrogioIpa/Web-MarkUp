Diabetes

Salmon and Asparagus

Serves: 2 Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: 20 min

Salmon provides omega-3 fatty acids, great for heart health, and the low-carb content makes it ideal for managing blood sugar.

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Nutritional Info (Per Serving):
Calories: ~400 kcal
Carbohydrates: 7g
Sugar: 2g
Protein: 35g
Fat: 25g
Fiber: 4g

Tools Needed:

  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Oven
  • Knife

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Arrange the asparagus around the salmon.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the salmon and asparagus, then sprinkle with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place lemon slices on top of the salmon fillets.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the salmon is fully cooked and the asparagus is tender.
  6. Serve immediately.
